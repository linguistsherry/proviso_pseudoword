You will read 4 different stories about aliens from different planets, far away from the Earth, and a statement characterizing an individual from each of those planets.

Afterwards, you will receive two new pieces of information, one at a time. Given what you were already told about the aliens, how surprised would you be to learn each of these new pieces of information?

For example, suppose you had previously been told that “Katie recently got divorced”. You might be very surprised if you later found out that she had never been never married, but not surprised at all to learn that Katie was unhappy with her marriage.

Your job is provide a rating for the statements on a scale from 0 to 100, with 0 being not surprised at all and 100 being totally surprised.
