As an adventurous journalist, you are travelling to 2 different planets far away from the Earth, to learn about alien cultures.

On each planet, you will first receive general information characterizing the aliens living on that planet. You will also interview a particular alien there who you've collected some information about.

Over the course of the interview, you will learn 2 new, separate things about this alien, one at a time. You job is to decide how surprised you would be to learn this new information, given what you previously learned about the aliens on this planet.

For example, suppose you have been told that “Katie recently got divorced”. When you interview Katie, you should be totally surprised if you found out that Katie had actually never been married, but not surprised at all to learn that Katie was unhappy with her marriage.

Your job is provide a rating on a scale from 0 to 100, with 0 being not surprised at all, 50 being somewhat surprised, and 100 being totally surprised.
